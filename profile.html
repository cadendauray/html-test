Lebron James, Kobe Bryant, and Steph Curry: The Main Attractions

Lebron James, Kobe Bryant, and Steph Curry are known as the top 3 basketball players of the past 5-10 years. Not only did these players command national attention while they were on the basketball court, they simultaneously grew the game globally and further increased revenue for the NBA.
